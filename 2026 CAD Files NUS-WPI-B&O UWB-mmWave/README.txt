Hello,

The folders here should be pretty self-explanatory about where each part is. I can't say the same about my naming convention for parts. The initial files I was given from the 2025 NUS research group are still in their original .zip files in the "2025 Files" Folder for reference. Many of these parts are extracted in the "UWB Calibration" folder, although they likely have edits made to them at that point, so I can't vouch for the backwards-compatibility of them.

In the "Point Stand" Folder are two subfolders: one for a stacking stand (which did get printed and used) and one for a telescoping stand (which did not get printed, as it would have taken far too long and too much money to do so). Inside the "UWB Calibration" Folder are two virtual assembly files. Being virtual, the links to the parts are broken and such do not update when the parts do, but otherwise contain the same information (as of this writing, 29 April 2026) as the assembly files inside the sub-folders.

I don't have much else to say, but if someone uses this repository and reads this note, good luck on your research/project!
